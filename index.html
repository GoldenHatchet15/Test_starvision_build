We are a small team of developers from Holberton school at Aguadilla, Puerto Rico. We are passionate about space and technology and we decided to create a simulation about our solar system that could help us explore and understand the wonders that we have outside our planet.

Why StarVision

There were many ways in which we could have approached the project, but in the end we decided to do it simple by just creating a simulation base of a gaming engine and do it in a more educational way rather than something like a game.
